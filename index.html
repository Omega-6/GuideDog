Privacy Notice
Camera Images Sent to Cloud
Every 3 seconds, GuideDog sends a photo from your camera to a cloud AI service (OpenAI and Anthropic) for advanced hazard detection (stairs, doors, wet floors). These images are processed in real time. Local object detection runs entirely on your device. By tapping Start, you consent to this image upload.
No Account Required
No personal data is collected. Images are not stored after analysis. You can use voice commands or the Scan button to trigger cloud analysis on demand instead.
